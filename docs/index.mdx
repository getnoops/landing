# Introduction

## Welcome to No_Ops!

No_Ops is the modern infrastructure platform for web developers. Rather than being slowed down by DevOps and worried about security and performance, No_Ops allows you to focus on your product.

## Getting starting

Start by [setting up your organisation](./getting-starting/setup-organisation.mdx) and [linking your AWS account](./getting-starting/link-aws-account.mdx), then [create your first services](./getting-starting/create-first-service.mdx) and [invite your team members](./getting-starting/invite-team-members.mdx)!
